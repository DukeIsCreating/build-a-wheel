Instructions

  1. Place the MAGNET-JIG in "WHEEL UP" position.
  2. Place each Magnet (MAG-1025) in turn on the MAGNET JIG to determine the correct polarity. Use a Tape Dot (TDSC) to mark the end of the magnet that faces up.
  3. Wrap each Magnet in a layer of 3/4" masking tape until the fit into the feet of the Demispider Shell is tight.
  4. Insert each Magnet, tape dot end first, into a leg of the Demispider. Ensure one final time that polarity is correct, and then push the magnet all of the way in until it is flush with the bottom of the leg.
