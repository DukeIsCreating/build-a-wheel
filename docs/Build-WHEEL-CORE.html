Build a Tabletop Game Wheel

Build Instructions

Assemble: Core Wheel, 3 Player Stations (x1)

WHEEL-CORE

Items Needed

Instructions

  1. Fit the Red Player Station on top of the Red Player Station bottom. Push down firmly.
  2. Repeat for Yellow and Blue Stations, placing them on their respective station bottom.
  3. Test the Demispider and the polarity of all magnets by ensuring that the Demispider magnetically attaches to each magnet in turn properly.
  4. Congratulations! You have completed the Base Wheel! Take it for a spin!
