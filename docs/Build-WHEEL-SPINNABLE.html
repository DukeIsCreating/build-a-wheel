Instructions

  1. With the help of a partner, carefully position the Wheel Deck so that the collar of the rotor is over the main bearing, and gently push the wheel down into place.
  2. The wheel should spin freely, should be supported by the struts, and the roller bearings on the struts should be tracking along the felt dampeners.
