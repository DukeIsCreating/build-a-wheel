Master Bill of Materials

The goal of this page is to provide a list of everything you need to build the various project stages of the wheel, and give you a breakdown of approximate costs involved. I have made every attempt to be as inclusive as possible, including costs of supplies, tools, and rounding up to next pack quantity, however, I do not represent these costs as 100% accurate, due to many factors outside of my control. Prices do not include sales tax.

Though I have listed brand names for the items that I sourced for my wheel build for convenience, this is not necessarily an endorsement of those brands. Branded item availability and price may fluctuate.

Colors listed are suggestions. You are, of course, free to customize colors to your liking.
